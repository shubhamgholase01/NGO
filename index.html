ABOUT US

A Real Story: Bruno's Journey

Bruno was found wandering near a garbage dump, malnourished and scared. His left leg was injured, and he had lost all hope in humanity.

After 8 weeks of intensive medical care, nutritious food, and endless love from our volunteers, Bruno started wagging his tail again. Today, he's the happiest dog in his forever home.

"He brings so much joy to our family. We can't imagine life without him."

— Bruno's Family
